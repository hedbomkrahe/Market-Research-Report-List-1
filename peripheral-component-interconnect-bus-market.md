<p><p>The Peripheral Component Interconnect (PCI) Bus is an internal computer bus for connecting peripheral devices to a computer's central processing unit. The PCI architecture has evolved over time, with advancements leading to PCI Express (PCIe), which offers significantly higher data transfer rates and improved efficiency.</p><p>The Peripheral Component Interconnect Bus Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.
