<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Printing Inks</li><li>Water-based Printing Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screen inks market encompasses various types, primarily solvent-based and water-based printing inks. Solvent-based inks utilize organic solvents for faster drying and are ideal for diverse substrates, offering vibrant colors and durability. They are commonly used in outdoor applications due to their weather resistance. Conversely, water-based inks are environmentally friendly, using water as a solvent, making them safer for indoor use and easier to clean. They are favored in textile printing and for eco-conscious businesses, prioritizing lower VOC emissions.</p></p>
