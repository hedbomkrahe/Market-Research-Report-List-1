<p><p>The animal cream market encompasses various types, including sweet cream, sour cream, reconstituted cream, dehydrated cream, and continuous mechanism cream. Sweet cream is fresh and unfermented, used in desserts and dishes. Sour cream undergoes fermentation, adding tanginess and thickness. Reconstituted cream is made by blending concentrated cream with water, while dehydrated cream has had moisture removed for extended shelf life and convenience. Continuous mechanism cream refers to a system that produces cream consistently and efficiently, ensuring quality and uniformity.</p></p>
