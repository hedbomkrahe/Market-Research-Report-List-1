<p><p>The fire protection piping system market comprises three main types: seamless steel pipes, welded steel tubes, and CPVC (Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride) pipes. Seamless steel pipes offer high strength and resistance to pressure, making them ideal for high-risk environments. Welded steel tubes provide flexibility in design and are cost-effective for various applications. CPVC pipes are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and ideal for low-pressure systems, offering ease of installation in residential and commercial fire protection settings. Each type serves distinct needs in fire safety.</p></p>
