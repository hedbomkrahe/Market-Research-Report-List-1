<p><strong>The Industrial Safety Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spring-loaded Safety Valves</li><li>Pilot-operated Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Safety Valves market primarily includes two types: spring-loaded safety valves and pilot-operated safety valves. Spring-loaded safety valves utilize a spring mechanism to maintain pressure within a system, opening when pressure exceeds a predefined limit. They are commonly used in various industries for overpressure protection. In contrast, pilot-operated safety valves use a pilot line to control the opening and closing mechanism under varying pressures, offering higher performance and efficiency in applications with fluctuating pressures. Both types ensure system safety and reliability.</p></p>
