<p><ul><li>2-D</li><li>3-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CAM software is categorized into 2D and 3D markets based on the dimensions they handle in manufacturing processes. The 2D CAM software focuses on flat or planar designs, suitable for tasks such as laser cutting, CNC milling, and engraving. In contrast, 3D CAM software manages more complex shapes, enabling advanced machining processes like 3D printing and sculpting. Both types cater to different industry needs, with 2D software often being simpler and more cost-effective, while 3D software offers greater design flexibility and precision.</p></p>
