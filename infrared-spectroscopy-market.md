<p><p>Infrared spectroscopy encompasses three main market segments: Near Infrared (NIR), Mid Infrared (Mid IR), and Far Infrared (Far IR). NIR spectroscopy is utilized for rapid, non-destructive analysis in fields like agriculture and pharmaceuticals, effective for identifying organic compounds. Mid IR is commonly used for molecular identification due to its sensitivity in detecting functional groups, making it valuable in chemical analysis. Far IR, with longer wavelengths, is less commonly used but can provide insights in material science and crystallography.</p></p>
