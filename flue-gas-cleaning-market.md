<p><p>The Flue Gas Cleaning market encompasses several technologies aimed at reducing pollutants from combustion processes. Dry Flue Gas Cleaning utilizes filters to capture particulates and absorbents to remove gases without water, enhancing efficiency. DeNOx/SCR systems employ catalysts to convert nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water vapor. Multi-stage Wet Scrubbing involves using liquid solutions to remove particulates and gases, often in multiple stages for greater effectiveness. Spray Absorption employs sprays to contact flue gases, facilitating the absorption of harmful substances while minimizing emissions.</p></p>
