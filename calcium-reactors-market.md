<p><p>Calcium reactors are essential in aquarium systems for maintaining calcium and alkalinity levels. The market is primarily segmented into single chamber and dual chamber structures. Single chamber reactors utilize one chamber for dissolving calcium carbonate media, offering simplicity and ease of use, ideal for smaller systems. In contrast, dual chamber reactors separate processes, enhancing efficiency by maintaining optimal conditions for calcium dissolution and CO2 addition, making them suitable for larger or more demanding aquatic environments. Both types cater to diverse user needs.</p></p>
