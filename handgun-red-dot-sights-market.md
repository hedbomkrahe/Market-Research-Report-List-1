<p><strong>The Handgun Red Dot Sights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SFP</li><li>FFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handgun red dot sights are categorized into two main types based on their reticle positioning: Second Focal Plane (SFP) and First Focal Plane (FFP). SFP sights maintain the reticle size regardless of magnification, providing consistent target acquisition at various distances. In contrast, FFP sights adjust the reticle size in relation to the target magnification, ensuring that the reticle remains proportionate at all zoom levels. Each type caters to different shooting styles and preferences, influencing accuracy and usability.</p></p>
