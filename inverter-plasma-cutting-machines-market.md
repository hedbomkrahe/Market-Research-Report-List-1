<p><p>Inverter plasma cutting machines are categorized into air-cooled and water-cooled types based on their cooling mechanisms. In contrast, water-cooled machines use a liquid coolant to manage heat more effectively, allowing for prolonged use and higher duty cycles, making them ideal for industrial applications. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the specific needs and operational demands of users.</p></p>
