<p><strong>The CdZnTe Radiation Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pixelated</li><li>Coplanar Grid</li><li>Planar-Type</li><li>Quasi-Hemispherical</li><li>Housed Quasi-Hemispherical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CdZnTe radiation detector market is categorized into several types. Pixelated detectors feature an array of individual pixels, enhancing spatial resolution. Coplanar grid detectors use interleaved electrodes for improved energy resolution. Planar-type detectors offer a simpler design, suitable for general applications. Quasi-hemispherical detectors provide a hemispherical configuration for improved detection efficiency. Housed quasi-hemispherical detectors are encapsulated for protection and enhanced performance in various environments. Each type caters to specific application needs across medical, industrial, and security sectors.</p></p>
