<p><p>The Metal Injection Molding (MIM) furnace market consists of two primary types: batch furnaces and continuous furnaces. Batch furnaces process multiple loads simultaneously, making them suitable for smaller production runs and varied part designs. Continuous furnaces, on the other hand, enable a steady flow of materials, optimizing production efficiency for high-volume applications. Both furnace types cater to different manufacturing needs, balancing between flexibility and productivity based on the scale and nature of the MIM production process.</p></p>
