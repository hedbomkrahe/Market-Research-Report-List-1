<p><p>The silicon diodes market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Gunn diodes are used in microwave frequency generation and oscillation. Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs) emit light when current flows, vital for displays and lighting. Schottky diodes, known for their low forward voltage drop, are widely utilized in power electronics for fast switching applications. Other diodes include Zener, photodiodes, and rectifiers, each playing crucial roles in electronics by ensuring efficient power management and signal processing across different sectors.</p></p>
