<p><ul><li>Resting ECG Devices</li><li>ECG Stress Test Devices</li><li>Holter Monitors</li><li>Implantable Cardiac Monitors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arrhythmia monitoring devices encompass various types tailored to track heart rhythms. Resting ECG devices capture electrical activity during a stationary state, while ECG stress test devices evaluate heart function under physical stress. Holter monitors provide extended monitoring over 24-48 hours, recording continuous heart rhythms. Implantable cardiac monitors are placed within the body for long-term tracking. Other devices may include event monitors and telemetry systems, each offering unique capabilities to diagnose and manage arrhythmias effectively.</p></p>
