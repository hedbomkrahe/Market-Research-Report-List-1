<p><p>Isostatically Pressed Graphite (IPG) is manufactured mainly through two methods: Cold Isostatic Pressing (CIP) and Vibration Molding. The CIP method involves applying uniform pressure to graphite powder in a flexible mold, resulting in a dense and homogeneous material ideal for high-performance applications. Vibration Molding, on the other hand, utilizes mechanical vibration to compact the graphite powder within a rigid mold, enhancing its flowability while achieving consistent density. Both methods cater to diverse industries, emphasizing efficiency and material integrity.</p></p>
