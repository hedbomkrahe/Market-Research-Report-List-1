<p><ul><li>Hot Stamping</li><li>Cold Stamping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car stamping involves shaping sheet metal into vehicle parts, primarily using two processes: hot stamping and cold stamping. Hot stamping uses heated metal sheets, allowing for complex shapes and enhanced material strength. It is ideal for producing high-strength components, often for safety applications. Cold stamping, on the other hand, uses room-temperature sheets, enabling quicker production cycles and lower costs, ideal for simpler shapes and high-volume manufacturing. Both methods play crucial roles in the automotive industry's efficiency and performance.</p></p>
