<p><p>Cushion foam pouches are categorized based on the density of polyethylene used in their production. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) provides strength and resistance against punctures, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. Middle Density Polyethylene (MDPE) offers a balance between strength and flexibility, suitable for medium-weight protection. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is more flexible and lightweight, perfect for delicate items. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) combines flexibility and strength, offering enhanced durability for various cushioning needs, ensuring optimal protection across various applications.</p></p>
