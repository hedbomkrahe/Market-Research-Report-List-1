<p><strong>The ECHO Cardiography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>M-mode</li><li>Two-dimensional (2-D, B-mode or real time)</li><li>Doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECHO cardiography encompasses three main types impacting the market: M-mode, Two-dimensional (2-D), and Doppler. M-mode provides a single-dimensional view, allowing precise measurements of cardiac structures over time. Two-dimensional echocardiography offers a real-time, comprehensive view of the heart, enabling detailed assessment of anatomy and function. Doppler echocardiography assesses blood flow dynamics and can measure velocity, contributing to the evaluation of heart diseases. Together, these modalities enhance diagnostic capabilities and drive growth in the echocardiography market.</p></p>
