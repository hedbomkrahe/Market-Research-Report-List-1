<p><strong>The Liver Biopsy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Percutaneous</li><li>Transjugular</li><li>Laparoscopic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liver biopsy market includes three primary types: percutaneous, transjugular, and laparoscopic. </p><p>Percutaneous biopsies involve needle insertion through the skin to obtain liver tissue, commonly used due to its minimal invasiveness. Transjugular biopsies are performed via the jugular vein, useful in patients with bleeding risks or ascites. Laparoscopic biopsies involve surgical intervention through small abdominal incisions, allowing direct visualization and sampling of the liver. Each method has specific indications, patient profiles, and advantages, shaping the evolving market landscape.</p></p>
