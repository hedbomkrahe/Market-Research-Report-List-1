<p><p>The 3D IC packaging solutions market includes several technologies such as Wire Bonding, TSV (Through-Silicon Via), and Fan-Out. Wire Bonding involves connecting chips using thin wires for electrical connections, making it cost-effective. TSV technology enables vertical connections between different layers of silicon, enhancing performance and density. Fan-Out packaging spreads die interconnections over a larger area, allowing for better thermal performance and reduced size. Additional methods, like micro-bumping and liquid crystal polymer packaging, also contribute to the diverse landscape of 3D IC solutions.</p></p>
