<p><p>Procurement software is available in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based procurement software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, regular updates, and lower upfront costs. On-premises procurement software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different business needs and preferences regarding flexibility and control.</p></p>
