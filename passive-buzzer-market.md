<p><p>The passive buzzer market can be categorized into two main types: stationary and pogo pin. Stationary buzzers are fixed devices designed for consistent sound generation, often used in alarms and timers. Pogo pin buzzers, on the other hand, feature spring-loaded contacts that allow for temporary connections, making them ideal for applications where a compact design and reliable performance are needed, such as in portable electronics. Both types serve essential roles in various electronic devices across industries.</p></p>
