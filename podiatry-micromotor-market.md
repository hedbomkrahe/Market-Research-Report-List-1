<p><ul><li>Brushed</li><li>Brushless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The podiatry micromotor market can be categorized into two main types: brushed and brushless micromotors. Brushed micromotors utilize brushes to transfer electricity to the rotor, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, but requiring more maintenance due to wear on the brushes. Brushless micromotors, on the other hand, employ electronic controllers for smoother operation and longer lifespan, reducing maintenance needs. Both types serve essential roles in podiatry practices, providing precision and efficiency for various foot care procedures.</p></p>
