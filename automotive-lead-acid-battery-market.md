<p><ul><li>VRLA Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Lead Acid Battery market comprises two main types: VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and feature valves to control gas release, making them safer and more convenient for modern vehicles. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, are traditional lead-acid batteries with liquid electrolyte that require regular maintenance, including topping off electrolyte levels. Both types have distinct applications, with VRLA commonly used in start-stop systems and advanced safety features.</p></p>
