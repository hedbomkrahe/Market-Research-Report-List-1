<p><p>The mechanical guards market comprises several types designed to enhance safety in industrial settings. Fixed machine guards provide permanent barriers to prevent access to hazardous areas, ensuring consistent protection. Interlocked machine guards are equipped with mechanisms that shut down machinery when the guard is opened, providing an added layer of safety. Adjustable machine guards offer flexibility, allowing operators to modify the guard's position based on varying machine operations, thus accommodating different tasks while maintaining safety standards. These types collectively contribute to workplace safety and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
