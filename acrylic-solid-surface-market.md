<p><strong>The Acrylic Solid Surface Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting Molding Solid Surface</li><li>Extrusion Molding Solid Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acrylic Solid Surface market is categorized into two primary types: Casting Molding and Extrusion Molding. Casting Molding Solid Surface involves pouring liquid acrylic into molds, allowing for intricate designs and custom shapes, often used for countertops and sinks. In contrast, Extrusion Molding Solid Surface involves forcing heated acrylic through a die to create continuous sheets or profiles, suitable for large-scale applications. Both methods offer durability and aesthetic versatility, catering to diverse architectural and consumer needs.</p></p>
