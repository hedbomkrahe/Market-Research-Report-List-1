<p><strong>The Automotive Third Brake Light Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas High Brake Light</li><li>LED High Brake Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive third brake light market primarily consists of two types: gas high brake lights and LED high brake lights. Gas high brake lights utilize incandescent bulbs and a gas-filled housing to produce illumination, offering moderate visibility but lower energy efficiency. In contrast, LED high brake lights use light-emitting diodes, providing brighter illumination, faster response times, and greater energy efficiency. Due to these advantages, the trend is shifting towards the adoption of LED technology in the automotive industry.</p></p>
