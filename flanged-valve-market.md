<p><p>The flanged valve market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Ball Valves** offer quick shut-off and low pressure drops, making them ideal for on/off applications. </p><p>2. **Globe Valves** are used for throttling flow, providing fine control. </p><p>3. **Butterfly Valves** are lightweight and compact, suitable for large volume flow with minimal resistance. </p><p>4. **Gate Valves** excel in fully opening or closing flow with minimal pressure loss. </p><p>5. **Others** encompass various specialized valves designed for specific industrial applications. These types cater to diverse fluid control needs in various sectors.</p></p>
