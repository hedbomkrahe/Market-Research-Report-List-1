<p><p>The single-ply membrane roofing market comprises various types, primarily TPO (Thermoplastic Olefin), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer). TPO is known for its energy efficiency and UV resistance, making it popular in commercial applications. PVC offers excellent durability and chemical resistance, ideal for areas with harsh environments. EPDM is favored for its longevity and flexibility, particularly in low-slope roofs. Other types include surfacing materials and hybrid systems, supplementing these primary offerings.</p></p>
