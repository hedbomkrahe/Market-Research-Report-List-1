<p><p>The MEMS Foundry Service market comprises two primary models: the Pure Play Model and the IDM (Integrated Device Manufacturer) Model. The Pure Play Model focuses solely on manufacturing MEMS devices for external clients, allowing customers to leverage specialized expertise without investing in production capabilities. Conversely, the IDM Model integrates both design and manufacturing, enabling companies to develop and produce MEMS internally, thereby maintaining tighter control over the entire process from conception to market delivery. Each model caters to different industry needs and strategies.</p></p>
