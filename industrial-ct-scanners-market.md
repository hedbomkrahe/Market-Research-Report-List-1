<p><ul><li>Micro Focus CT</li><li>High Energy CT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial CT scanners are categorized into two main types: Micro Focus CT and High Energy CT. Micro Focus CT utilizes lower energy levels and high-resolution imaging, making it ideal for examining small, intricate parts and detecting minute defects in materials. In contrast, High Energy CT scanners operate at elevated energy levels, enabling the scanning of larger components and denser materials, such as those found in aerospace and automotive industries. Both types serve crucial roles in quality control, research, and development applications.</p></p>
