<p><p>The urban gas market includes three primary types: natural gas, manufactured gas, and liquefied petroleum gas (LPG). Natural gas, sourced from underground deposits, is widely used for heating and cooking. Manufactured gas, produced from coal or biomass, has historical significance but is less common today. LPG, derived from petroleum refining and natural gas processing, is versatile, portable, and often used in areas lacking natural gas infrastructure. Each gas type caters to diverse energy needs within urban environments.</p></p>
