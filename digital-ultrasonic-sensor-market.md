<p><strong>The Digital Ultrasonic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Retro-Reflective Sensor</li><li>Through Beam Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital ultrasonic sensor market features two primary types: retro-reflective sensors and through beam sensors. Retro-reflective sensors utilize a transmitter and receiver in a single unit, bouncing ultrasonic waves off a reflective surface to detect objects. They are ideal for short-range applications and offer ease of installation. In contrast, through beam sensors consist of separate transmitter and receiver units, requiring direct alignment. They provide a reliable solution for detecting objects over longer distances, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
