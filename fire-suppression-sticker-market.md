<p><p>The fire suppression sticker market offers innovative solutions utilizing inert gases and aerogel. Inert gas-type stickers release gases like nitrogen or argon to displace oxygen, effectively smothering flames while ensuring safety for the environment. On the other hand, aerogel-type stickers utilize advanced materials that can absorb heat and inhibit flame spread, providing passive fire protection. Both types contribute to enhanced safety measures in various settings, from commercial buildings to vehicles, offering tailored solutions for fire risk management.</p></p>
