<p><p>The semiconductor wastewater treatment market is categorized into three main types. Fluorinated wastewater contains contaminants from the use of fluorinated chemicals in manufacturing processes, requiring specialized treatment methods to prevent environmental harm. Organic wastewater includes a variety of organic compounds that arise during production and necessitates biological or chemical treatment processes. Metal ion wastewater involves the presence of heavy metals, requiring techniques like precipitation, ion exchange, or advanced filtration to mitigate potential toxicity before discharge.</p></p>
