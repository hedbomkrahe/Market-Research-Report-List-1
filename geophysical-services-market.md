<p><p>The geophysical services market is primarily categorized into aerial-based and land-based survey sectors. In contrast, land-based surveys involve ground-based techniques, using equipment like seismic sensors and magnetometers to obtain detailed subsurface information, making them ideal for targeted investigations and higher resolution data in localized areas. Both methods are essential for resource exploration, environmental assessments, and infrastructure development.</p></p>
