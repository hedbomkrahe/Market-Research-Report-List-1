<p><ul><li>Dry Premixed Plasters</li><li>Wet Premixed Plasters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The premixed plasters market is divided into two primary types: dry premixed plasters and wet premixed plasters. Dry premixed plasters are powdered formulations that require water for mixing before application, offering convenience and longer shelf life. They are often used for interior and exterior wall finishes. Wet premixed plasters, on the other hand, are ready-to-use mixtures delivered in liquid form, providing ease of application and consistent texture, making them suitable for quick and efficient projects in construction and renovation.</p></p>
