<p><p>The MgO (Magnesium Oxide) panel market is categorized based on thickness into three types: Thin (below 8 mm), Medium (8-15 mm), and Thick (above 15 mm). Thin panels are lightweight, ideal for interior applications like partitions and ceilings. Medium panels balance strength and weight, suitable for wall cladding and fireproofing. Thick panels offer superior durability and load-bearing capacity, often used in structural and exterior applications. Each type caters to specific construction needs, ensuring versatility across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
