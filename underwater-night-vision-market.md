<p><p>The underwater night vision market comprises three primary types: Image Intensifier Night Vision enhances low-light visibility by amplifying existing light, making it effective in dark water conditions. Thermal Image Night Vision detects temperature differences, allowing users to see heat signatures of objects, ideal for locating marine life or submerged items. Digital Night Vision utilizes advanced sensors to capture and process images in low light, providing a clearer and more detailed view, suitable for surveillance and navigation in dark underwater environments.</p></p>
