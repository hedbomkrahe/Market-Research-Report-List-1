<p><p>The healthcare packaging market is categorized into primary and secondary packaging. Primary packaging refers to the materials that directly contain and protect the pharmaceutical product, such as vials, syringes, and blister packs. It ensures product safety, integrity, and user convenience. Secondary packaging includes outer packaging that groups primary packages for distribution and sale, such as boxes and cartons. It offers additional protection, branding, and information, playing a crucial role in logistics and marketing within the healthcare industry.</p></p>
