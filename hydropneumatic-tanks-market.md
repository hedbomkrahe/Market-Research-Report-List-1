<p><p>Hydropneumatic tanks are crucial in water supply systems, maintaining pressure and ensuring a steady flow. The market is primarily segmented into bladder type and diaphragm type tanks. Bladder type tanks feature a rubber bladder that separates air and water, allowing for efficient pressure maintenance. In contrast, diaphragm type tanks use a flexible diaphragm to achieve similar functions. Each type serves specific applications, with bladder types often preferred for larger systems, while diaphragm types are typically utilized in smaller installations.</p></p>
