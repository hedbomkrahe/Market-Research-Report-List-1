<p><strong>The Food Grade Flax Seed Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Squeezing Method</li><li>Leaching Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food grade flaxseed oil is primarily produced through two methods: squeezing and leaching. The squeezing method involves mechanically pressing flaxseeds to extract oil, preserving its natural nutrients and flavor. This method yields a high-quality oil rich in omega-3 fatty acids. In contrast, the leaching method uses solvents to dissolve and extract oil from the seeds, often resulting in a lower-quality product with fewer nutrients. Both methods cater to different market segments, influenced by consumer preferences for quality and processing standards.</p></p>
