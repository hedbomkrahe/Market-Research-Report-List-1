<p><p>The plastic resins market includes various types, each with distinct properties and applications. **High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE)** is durable, chemical-resistant, and used in bottles, pipes, and packaging. **Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE)** is flexible and lightweight, ideal for films, bags, and coatings. **Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET, PETE)** is strong, transparent, and commonly used in beverage bottles and food containers. **Polypropylene (PP)** is heat-resistant and used in automotive parts, textiles, and packaging. **Polystyrene** is rigid or foamed, used in insulation and disposable products. **Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC, Vinyl)** is versatile, used in construction, pipes, and medical devices. **Others** include niche resins like ABS and polycarbonate.</p></p>
