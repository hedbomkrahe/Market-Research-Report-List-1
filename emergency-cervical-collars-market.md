<p><p>The Emergency Cervical Collars market primarily includes two types: soft cervical collars and rigid cervical collars. Soft cervical collars are designed for mild support and comfort, providing flexibility while limiting excessive neck movement, often used for minor injuries. In contrast, rigid cervical collars offer enhanced immobilization, typically used in emergency situations to stabilize severe neck injuries. Both types are critical for ensuring appropriate spinal alignment and preventing further injury during transportation or treatment in medical settings.</p></p>
