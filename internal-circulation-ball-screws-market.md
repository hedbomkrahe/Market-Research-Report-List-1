<p><p>The internal circulation ball screws market is primarily segmented into single nut and double nut types. Single nut ball screws feature a single nut that utilizes recirculating ball bearings for efficient motion transfer, making them suitable for applications requiring compact designs and lower loads. In contrast, double nut ball screws consist of two nuts, enhancing rigidity and load capacity, ideal for high-load or precision applications. The choice between these types hinges on specific performance needs, including load requirements and space constraints.</p></p>
