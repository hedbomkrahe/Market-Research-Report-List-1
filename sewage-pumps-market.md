<p><strong>The Sewage Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Day Sewage Pumps</li><li>Submersible Sewage Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sewage pump market consists primarily of two types: day sewage pumps and submersible sewage pumps. Day sewage pumps are typically surface-mounted and designed for above-ground installation, facilitating easy maintenance and monitoring. They function continuously, transferring sewage from holding tanks to processing facilities. In contrast, submersible sewage pumps operate submerged in the fluid, making them ideal for deep pits or basements. These pumps efficiently handle large volumes of waste while reducing the risk of odors and noise, ensuring effective sewage management.</p></p>
