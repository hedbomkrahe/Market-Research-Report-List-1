<p><strong>The Coolant Flow Control Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Way</li><li>3 Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coolant flow control valves are essential components in managing temperature and fluid flow in various systems. The market primarily consists of two types: 2-way and 3-way valves. </p><p>2-way valves permit fluid flow in one direction, allowing for simple on/off control, ideal for straightforward applications. Conversely, 3-way valves can divert or mix coolant in multiple paths, providing greater flexibility and control for complex systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing system efficiency and preventing overheating across industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
