<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The latex tourniquet market is divided into two main types: manual and pneumatic. Manual latex tourniquets are operated by hand, allowing healthcare providers to easily control pressure and application during procedures. In contrast, pneumatic tourniquets utilize compressed air for inflation, providing precise pressure control and reducing the risk of complications. Both types serve critical roles in medical settings, facilitating blood flow management during surgeries and blood draws, with each offering distinct advantages based on specific clinical needs.</p></p>
