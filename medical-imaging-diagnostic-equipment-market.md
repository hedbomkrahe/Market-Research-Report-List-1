<p><p>The medical imaging diagnostic equipment market comprises various technologies, including Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), Computed Tomography (CT), Ultrasound (USG), and Nuclear Medicine Imaging (NMI). MRI utilizes strong magnets and radio waves to create detailed images of soft tissues. CT scans use X-rays to produce cross-sectional views of the body. Ultrasound employs sound waves to visualize internal organs and monitor pregnancies. NMI involves radioactive substances to diagnose and treat conditions through imaging functions, highlighting the diverse applications of diagnostic imaging in healthcare.</p></p>
