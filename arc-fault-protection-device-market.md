<p><p>Arc-fault protection devices (AFPDs) are crucial for enhancing electrical safety. The market can be categorized into two main types: Bypass Feeder Type and Output Circuit Type. Bypass Feeder Type AFPDs are designed to protect feeders that supply multiple circuits, ensuring comprehensive coverage against electrical arcs. In contrast, Output Circuit Type AFPDs provide protection on a per-circuit basis, focusing on individual branch circuits to detect and mitigate arc faults. Both types enhance safety and help prevent electrical fires, fulfilling regulatory requirements.</p></p>
