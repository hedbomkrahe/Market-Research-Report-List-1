<p><p>The portable printer market consists of three primary types: thermal printers, inkjet printers, and impact printers. Thermal printers use heat to produce images on specially coated paper, making them ideal for labels and receipts. Inkjet printers spray liquid ink onto paper, offering high-quality color printing and versatility for various media. Impact printers rely on physical impact, using a mechanism similar to typewriters, to transfer ink to the paper, making them suitable for multi-part forms and carbon-copy printing. Each type serves distinct needs across different industries.</p></p>
