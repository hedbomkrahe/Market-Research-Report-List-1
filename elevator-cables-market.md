<p><ul><li>TVVB</li><li>RVV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elevator cables market primarily includes two types: TVVB and RVV cables. TVVB cables are designed for vertical and horizontal uses, characterized by their tough insulation and flexibility, making them ideal for elevators in buildings. RVV cables, on the other hand, are multi-core and flexible, suitable for applications requiring high flexibility and durable performance. Both types are essential for ensuring safe and efficient elevator operations, catering to different installation environments and technical requirements.</p></p>
