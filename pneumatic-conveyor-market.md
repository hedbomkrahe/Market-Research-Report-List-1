<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure System</li><li>Vacuum System</li><li>Combination System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic conveyor market encompasses three primary types: Positive Pressure Systems, Vacuum Systems, and Combination Systems. Positive Pressure Systems utilize compressed air to push materials through pipelines, ideal for transporting lightweight items over long distances. Vacuum Systems rely on suction to pull materials, best suited for fragile products sensitive to pressure. Combination Systems integrate both methods, offering flexibility to handle various materials efficiently. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, optimizing the transport of bulk materials across diverse applications.</p></p>
