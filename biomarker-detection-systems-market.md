<p><p>The biomarker detection systems market comprises multimode and single mode microplate readers. Multimode microplate readers offer versatility by enabling various detection methods, including fluorescence, luminescence, and absorbance in a single device. This flexibility caters to diverse experimental requirements. In contrast, single mode microplate readers specialize in one detection method, providing high sensitivity and specificity for targeted applications. Both systems are essential in clinical diagnostics and research, facilitating the identification and quantification of biomarkers for disease monitoring and drug development.</p></p>
