<p><p>Digital Subtraction Angiography (DSA) equipment is a medical imaging technology used primarily to visualize blood vessels and identify abnormalities in the vascular system. It employs a digital imaging technique that subtracts pre-contrast images from post-contrast images, enhancing the visibility of blood vessels while minimizing background noise. DSA is vital in interventional radiology, cardiology, and neurology for diagnosing and treating various conditions, such as blockages and vascular malformations.</p><p>The DSA equipment market is poised for significant growth, driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in imaging technology, and a growing preference for minimally invasive procedures.
