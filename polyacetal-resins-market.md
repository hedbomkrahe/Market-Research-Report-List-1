<p><p>Polyacetal resins, also known as polyoxymethylene (POM), are high-performance engineering thermoplastics known for their strength, stiffness, and chemical resistance. The market is divided into two main types: **Acetal Homopolymer Resins** and **Acetal Copolymer Resins**. Homopolymers offer higher mechanical strength, stiffness, and thermal stability, making them ideal for precision parts. Copolymers provide better chemical resistance, lower friction, and improved processability, suited for applications requiring durability and flexibility. Both types are widely used in automotive, electronics, and consumer goods industries.</p></p>
