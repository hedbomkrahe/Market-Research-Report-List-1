<p><p>The Shipping Sacks market comprises three primary types: paper-based, woven-based, and plastic-based sacks. </p><p>Paper-based sacks are eco-friendly, biodegradable, and suitable for lightweight materials. Woven-based sacks, made from polypropylene or similar materials, offer high durability and are ideal for heavy goods like fertilizers and grains. Plastic-based sacks, typically made from polyethylene, are moisture-resistant and versatile for various products but raise environmental concerns due to their non-biodegradable nature. Each type serves distinct needs in packaging and transportation.</p></p>
