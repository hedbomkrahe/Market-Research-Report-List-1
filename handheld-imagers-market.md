<p><p>Handheld imagers encompass various technologies designed for different applications. Stud finders detect wooden or metal beams behind walls for construction projects. Infrared (IR) scanners visualize temperature variations, aiding in energy audits and electrical inspections. Millimeter wave scanners use high-frequency waves for security screening, identifying concealed objects without harmful radiation. Microbolometers are sensitive thermal detectors that capture temperature images, commonly employed in surveillance and firefighting. Each type serves unique purposes, enhancing safety, efficiency, and monitoring capabilities across diverse fields.</p></p>
