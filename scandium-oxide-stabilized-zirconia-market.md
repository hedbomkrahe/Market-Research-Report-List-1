<p><ul><li>Sol-Gel Method</li><li>Co-Precipitation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scandium Oxide Stabilized Zirconia (ScSZ) is produced using various methods, primarily the Sol-Gel and Co-Precipitation methods. The Sol-Gel method involves the transition of a solution into a solid gel phase, allowing for uniform dispersion of scandium oxide within zirconia, enhancing its properties. The Co-Precipitation method entails the simultaneous precipitation of zirconium and scandium ions, resulting in well-mixed precursors. Both methods contribute to the development of high-performance materials for applications in fuel cells, ceramics, and electronics due to improved mechanical and thermal stability.</p></p>
