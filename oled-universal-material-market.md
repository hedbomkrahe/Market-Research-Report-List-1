<p><p>The OLED universal material market comprises several key types: ETL (Electron Transport Layer), which facilitates electron movement; EIL (Electron Injection Layer), aiding in electron entry; HIL (Hole Injection Layer), promoting hole entry; HTL (Hole Transport Layer), enabling hole movement; and Other materials that support diverse functions like light emission and substrate protection. Each category plays a critical role in enhancing OLED performance, efficiency, and longevity, contributing collectively to the advancement of display and lighting technologies.</p></p>
