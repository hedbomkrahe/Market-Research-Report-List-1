<p><p>Mononitrobenzene (MNB) is an organic compound with the chemical formula C6H5NO2, commonly used as an intermediate in the production of aniline, which is further utilized in the manufacture of dyes, pharmaceuticals, rubber chemicals, and pesticides. It is produced through the nitration of benzene using a mixture of nitric and sulfuric acids.
