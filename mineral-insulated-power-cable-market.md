<p><p>The Mineral Insulated Power Cable market comprises two primary types: Single Core and Multi Core cables. Single Core cables contain one conductor and are primarily used for straightforward applications requiring high-temperature resistance and low fire risk. In contrast, Multi Core cables consist of multiple conductors bundled together, offering flexibility and ease of installation for complex electrical systems. Both types utilize mineral insulation, providing excellent durability and protection against moisture, chemicals, and mechanical damage, making them ideal for critical power applications.</p></p>
