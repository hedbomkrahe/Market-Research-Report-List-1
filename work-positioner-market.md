<p><p>The work positioner market is categorized into three types based on load capacity: light duty, medium duty, and heavy duty. Light duty positioners are designed for tasks involving lighter loads, typically under 500 pounds, suitable for assembly and packaging. Medium duty positioners handle loads between 500 and 1,500 pounds, ideal for more demanding tasks in manufacturing. Heavy duty positioners can manage over 1,500 pounds, perfect for sectors like construction and heavy machinery assembly, offering robust support for substantial workloads.</p></p>
