<p><ul><li>Single Phase EMI/EMC Filters</li><li>Three Phase EMI/EMC Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMI/EMC filters market is categorized into two main types: single-phase and three-phase filters. Single-phase EMI/EMC filters are typically used in residential and light commercial applications, protecting sensitive electronic devices from electromagnetic interference (EMI) in circuits with a single current phase. In contrast, three-phase EMI/EMC filters cater to industrial and heavy machinery applications, addressing EMI in complex, three-phase power systems. Both types ensure compliance with electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) standards, enhancing the performance and reliability of electronic equipment.</p></p>
