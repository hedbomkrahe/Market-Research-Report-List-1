<p><p>Furan resins are thermosetting polymers derived from furan compounds, widely used for their excellent chemical and heat resistance. **Furfuryl Alcohol Resin** is produced by polymerizing furfuryl alcohol, offering high corrosion resistance, making it ideal for chemical tanks and linings. **Furfural Resin** is derived from furfural, used in adhesives and coatings due to its durability. **Bran Ketone Resin** and **Branone Formaldehyde Resin** are specialized types, combining furan with ketones or formaldehyde, respectively, enhancing mechanical strength and thermal stability for industrial applications like foundry binders and composites.</p></p>
