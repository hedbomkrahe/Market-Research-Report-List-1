<p><ul><li>Non-contact Position Sensor</li><li>Contact Position Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LVDT linear position sensor market is divided into non-contact and contact position sensors. Non-contact position sensors utilize electromagnetic principles to measure linear displacement without physical contact, offering high durability and long lifespan. In contrast, contact position sensors operate through direct physical interaction with the moving surface, providing precise measurements but potentially facing wear over time. Both types are essential in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, where accurate position sensing is critical for operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
