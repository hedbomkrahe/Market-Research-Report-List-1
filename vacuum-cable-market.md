<p><ul><li>High Vacuum</li><li>UHV</li><li>XHV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum cable market is categorized into three main types based on the degree of vacuum achieved: High Vacuum, Ultra High Vacuum (UHV), and Extreme High Vacuum (XHV). High Vacuum refers to pressures below 10^-3 torr, typically used in industrial applications. UHV operates between 10^-3 to 10^-9 torr and is essential for research facilities and particle accelerators. XHV extends beyond 10^-9 torr, applied in advanced scientific experiments and semiconductor manufacturing, requiring ultra-pure environments for optimal results.</p></p>
